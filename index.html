The Science Behind CoolSculpting CoolSculpting is a non-invasive fat reduction treatment based on the scientific process of cryolipolysis. This technique was discovered by Harvard scientists who found that fat cells freeze at higher temperatures than surrounding tissues. When exposed to controlled cooling, the fat cells crystallize, break down, and eventually die. Over time, the body naturally processes and eliminates these dead fat cells, resulting in a more sculpted appearance. Unlike traditional weight loss, which only shrinks fat cells, CoolSculpting permanently removes them. The results are long-lasting, provided you maintain a healthy lifestyle through proper diet and exercise to prevent new fat accumulation.
